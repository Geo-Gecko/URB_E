Mobile Money Access

GeoGecko mapped all 399,388 buildings in Kampala. We also mapped all 10,199 mobile money agents. We then calculated the distance between each building and the nearest mobile money agent. The result is the pretty map you see here.

But this is more than just a pretty map!

This data can provide tangible and actionable information. We are using this data to asses access to utilities, identify slums, understand city expensaion and much more
